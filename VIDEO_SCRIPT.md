# 🧠 SOUDE — Video Script
### Team SOUDE · Br41n.IO Hackathon · Max 5 minutes

---

> **TONE:** Cinematic, urgent, wonder-inducing. Think: product launch meets TED talk.
> **MUSIC:** Low, pulsing ambient electronic (e.g., Hans Zimmer-esque) — builds through demo, swells at the end.
> **VISUAL PALETTE:** Dark backgrounds, electric cyan / blue glow, minimal text on screen. The UI's own aesthetic is the brand.

---

## ▶ SEGMENT 1 — THE HOOK (0:00 – 0:25)

**[VISUAL]** Black screen. Silence.

**[ON SCREEN — white text, appears word by word]**
> *"Every 39 seconds…*
> *a data breach happens."*

**[VISUAL]** Cut to: rapid montage — news headlines, lock icons breaking, padlocks falling, spinning "Forgot your password?" dialogs. Fast, disorienting.

**[NARRATION — calm, deliberate]**
> "In 2024 alone, over **8 billion passwords** were exposed.
> Phishing. Keyloggers. Database dumps.
> The system we built our digital lives on… is fundamentally broken."

**[VISUAL]** Hard cut to black again.

**[ON SCREEN — single line, glowing cyan]**
> *"What if the password was inside your brain?"*

---

## ▶ SEGMENT 2 — THE PROBLEM (0:25 – 0:55)

**[VISUAL]** Person typing a password. Hacker watching. Password reused across sites. All the clichés — because they're real.

**[NARRATION]**
> "Every password system shares the same fatal flaw:
> **the secret has to leave your head.**
>
> You type it. It travels. It gets stored. It gets stolen.
>
> What if authentication never required you to *reveal* anything at all?"

**[VISUAL]** Slow zoom into a human brain scan — real fMRI imagery. Neurons firing.

**[NARRATION]**
> "The answer has been hiding inside us all along.
> It's called the **P300**."

---

## ▶ SEGMENT 3 — THE NEUROSCIENCE (0:55 – 1:55)

**[VISUAL]** EEG waveform animation — clean, minimal. A spike appears at exactly 300 ms.

**[NARRATION]**
> "The P300 is an involuntary electrical signal your brain produces —
> approximately 300 milliseconds after you **recognize something meaningful**."

**[VISUAL]** Show the oddball paradigm concept: a stream of grey shapes, then suddenly — a vivid red star. Person's face lights up slightly. The EEG line spikes.

**[NARRATION]**
> "It's the 'oh, I know that' signal.
> And here's what makes it extraordinary:"

**[ON SCREEN — three lines appear, one at a time]**
> ✦ **Involuntary** — you cannot suppress it
> ✦ **Individual** — unique to each person's brain
> ✦ **Uncopyable** — it cannot be replayed or forged

**[NARRATION]**
> "If 20 images flash before you, your brain will spike —
> only for the ones you chose as your password.
> Not because you think about it.
> Because you **recognize** them.
>
> You can't fake recognition. Neuroscience doesn't negotiate."

**[VISUAL]** Real EEG signal — clean P300 peak visible on Cz, Pz, Oz channels.

---

## ▶ SEGMENT 4 — THE DEMO (1:55 – 3:30)

**[VISUAL]** Cut to: team member putting on the **Unicorn Hybrid Black** EEG headset. Confident, almost casual.

**[NARRATION]**
> "This is Soude. Built in 36 hours. Running on a real 8-channel EEG headset."

**[VISUAL]** Screen capture of the app's **Home Screen** — the dark UI, glowing cyan SOUDE title, signal quality dots turning green one by one.

**[NARRATION]**
> "The signal quality monitor confirms electrode contact.
> No gel shortcuts. Real neuroscience."

**[VISUAL]** Cut to **Enrollment Screen** — the 5×4 grid of images. Person clicks three images: ★ STAR, ♦ DIAMOND, ● CIRCLE. Each lights up with a green border.

**[NARRATION]**
> "Enrollment takes seconds. The user privately selects three images —
> their **mental password**. No one else sees the choice.
> Not even us."

**[VISUAL]** Person clicks **BEGIN NEURAL SCAN**. The stimulus screen takes over — full screen black, images flashing.

**[NARRATION]**
> "The oddball paradigm begins.
> 20 images. Five repetitions each. Flashing at 500 milliseconds per image.
>
> The user does nothing but **think**.
> Mentally count the appearances of their three password images.
> That's it."

**[VISUAL]** Tight shot on the person's face — calm, focused. EEG headset visible. Images reflected in their eyes.

**[VISUAL]** Progress through the sequence. We see the dark distractor images — then suddenly the vivid red STAR flashes. The person's expression shifts, subtly.

**[NARRATION]**
> "Somewhere in that brain, right now,
> a P300 is firing.
> Quietly. Involuntarily. Perfectly."

**[VISUAL]** Scan completes. Brief pause. Then —

**[VISUAL]** **ACCESS GRANTED** fills the screen in glowing green. 🔓 emoji. The vault opens.

**[NARRATION — dropping to near-whisper]**
> "Access granted."

**[VISUAL]** The **Vault Screen** — password cards revealed one by one as the eye button is tapped. Smooth, satisfying.

**[NARRATION]**
> "No keyboard. No PIN. No face scan.
> Just a thought."

---

## ▶ SEGMENT 5 — THE ANTI-SPOOF (3:30 – 3:55)

**[VISUAL]** Different person sits down at the same computer. Same headset. They've seen the password images — STAR, DIAMOND, CIRCLE.

**[NARRATION]**
> "But what if someone watches you? What if they know your three images?"

**[VISUAL]** Attacker runs the scan. **ACCESS DENIED** — red, definitive.

**[NARRATION]**
> "Without your neural signature, the images mean nothing.
>
> You can steal a password. You can copy a fingerprint.
> You **cannot steal recognition**.
>
> There is no hash to crack. No database to breach.
> The key lives only in the electrochemical patterns of a single brain —
> and it dies with the session."

---

## ▶ SEGMENT 6 — THE FUTURE (3:55 – 4:40)

**[VISUAL]** Abstract montage — hospital, bank vault, military access point, a person with motor disabilities, a child with no hands. Each scene: a person looks at a screen. Access granted.

**[NARRATION]**
> "Imagine a world where:
>
> A paralyzed patient authenticates to their medical records — with thought alone.
>
> A bank vault opens based on who you *are* neurologically, not what you carry.
>
> High-security facilities grant access without ever asking for a credential that can be tortured, coerced, or stolen.
>
> And for the **200 million people worldwide** with motor impairments —
> a password system they can actually use."

**[VISUAL]** Pull back to show the Soude UI — it looks small now against this vision.

**[NARRATION]**
> "We built a proof of concept in 36 hours.
> The neuroscience is 40 years old.
> The hardware is consumer-grade.
>
> The only thing that was missing… was someone to connect the dots."

## 📋 PRODUCTION NOTES

### Shot List Checklist
- [ ] Headset being put on (close-up, confident)
- [ ] Signal quality dots turning green on home screen
- [ ] Enrollment: clicking 3 images
- [ ] Full stimulus sequence (30-60 sec capture — edit down to ~20 sec)
- [ ] Close-up of person's face during scan
- [ ] ACCESS GRANTED screen
- [ ] Vault screen with passwords revealed
- [ ] ACCESS DENIED with a different person (anti-spoof demo)
- [ ] Team shot at the end

### Narration Tips
- Record narration separately from screen capture for maximum control
- Speak at ~130 words/minute — slower than you think sounds right
- The pauses are part of the script — let them breathe

### B-roll suggestions
- Real EEG waveform from `erp_viewer.py` running during the scan
- Close-up of electrodes / gel application (shows rigor)
- Terminal log scrolling (shows technical depth)
- The signal quality indicator dots on the home screen
